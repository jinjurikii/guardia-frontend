Our Story

A Dream from Italy

In 2000, Vito and Giovanna Bua opened the doors of Original Italian Pizza with a simple promise — to bring the authentic flavors of their homeland to Sunbury.

Every sauce, every bread, every recipe comes from Vito's mother's kitchen. No shortcuts. No compromises.

The Tradition

Carried by Family

Twenty-five years later, the tradition continues. Rosario Bua, their son, now leads the kitchen — keeping the family recipes alive while honoring the standards his parents set.

When you eat here, you're eating at our family table.

Today

Same Love. Same Recipes.

From the homemade pasta fagioli to the Italian wedding soup, every dish tells a story of where we came from — and why we stayed.
