Our Story

Two Artists.
One Vision.
Every Shade.

Shades of Dimension was born from a shared belief: that exceptional hair isn't about following trends — it's about understanding the person in the chair.

Lacy and Shaun bring complementary artistry to every appointment. Color that looks like it grew from your roots. Cuts that move with you, not against you. Extensions that disappear. Treatments that actually heal.

Using organic products and modern technique, they've quietly built a reputation that reaches far beyond Selinsgrove. Walk-ins are welcome. But once you experience the difference, you'll be booking ahead.
