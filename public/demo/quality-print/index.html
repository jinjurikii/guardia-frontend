Our Story

The Shop on Duke Street

For over ninety years, Quality Print Shop has been right here on Duke Street in Northumberland. Through world wars and recessions, through the digital revolution and everything after — we've kept the presses running.

The job hasn't changed much. Someone walks in with an idea — a business card, a wedding invitation, a banner for the church fundraiser — and we make it real. Ink on paper. Done right, done fast, at a fair price.

We're not the biggest shop. We're not the flashiest. But when your business cards need to be perfect and you need them Tuesday — you know where to find us.
