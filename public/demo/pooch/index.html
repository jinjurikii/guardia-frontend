The Artist

30 Years of Craft.
One Passion.

It started at Sunbury Animal Hospital — just grooming one day a week. But one day a week became every day. The dogs deserved it, and Shawnee couldn't stop.

In September 2019, she opened The Painted Pooch on Market Street. By the end of the first year, business had tripled. Word got around — not just in Sunbury, but internationally.

Published in "I Fashion Pet" magazine. OPAWZ Certified creative groomer. 30+ years of experience. But if you ask her, it's simple:

"When it comes to grooming, it's like art. That's why we called it The Painted Pooch." — Shawnee, Owner & Artist

Barkbury

Giving Back to the City
That Gave Us Everything

Our clients kept saying Sunbury needs a place for their dogs to run free. A real park. Not a parking lot, not a side street — somewhere safe.

So we're building it. Barkbury — a dog park for Sunbury, by Sunbury. Because this community deserves a safe space for every wagging tail.
